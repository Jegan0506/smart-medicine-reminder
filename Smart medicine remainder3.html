The Problem

Forgetting Medicines Is More Dangerous Than You Think

Medication non-adherence causes nearly 125,000 deaths and costs the healthcare system $100+ billion annually.

🧠

Memory Lapses

Busy schedules and cognitive overload make it easy to forget dose timings, especially for multi-medication regimens.

⚠️

Health Complications

Missed doses can lead to disease progression, treatment failure, emergency hospitalizations, and worsened outcomes.

📉

No Dose Tracking

Without a tracking system, patients and doctors have no visibility into adherence history or missed medications.

🕐

Timing Complexity

Multiple medicines at different times, some with food or empty stomach requirements — impossible to remember manually.
